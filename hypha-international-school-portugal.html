Hypha International School is one of the most distinctive new schools to open in Portugal. Situated in the Sintra-Cascais Natural Park, on a 7,000m² estate of private gardens and wooded areas in Colares, Sintra, Hypha combines the Cambridge curriculum with Forest School principles — a rare and compelling combination that is almost unheard of in international education in Portugal.

Children spend a minimum of 3 hours per day outdoors — climbing trees, tending animals, gardening, and exploring the natural environment around them. This is not a separate "outdoor programme" but woven into the school's entire educational philosophy. Academic rigour and deep nature connection are treated as complementary, not competing.

The school is small and intentional — 56 students in total, up to 16 per class, with 17 nationalities represented. Teaching is bilingual in English and Portuguese. Lunch and school transport are both included in the fees, which is unusual for international schools in Portugal. Hypha currently serves ages 3 to 10 (Nursery through Grade 5).

Frequently asked questions
What makes Hypha different from other international schools in Portugal?
Hypha combines the Cambridge curriculum with Forest School principles — a combination that is almost unique in Portugal. Children spend a minimum of 3 hours per day outdoors in the Sintra-Cascais Natural Park, caring for animals, gardening, and exploring the natural environment. Academic learning and nature connection are treated as inseparable.
What is a Forest School?
Forest School is an educational approach originating in Scandinavia that uses outdoor environments as the primary learning space. Children develop confidence, resilience, creativity, and independence through regular, sustained contact with nature. At Hypha, this is combined with the structured academic rigour of Cambridge — children get both.
How much does Hypha International School cost?
Annual tuition ranges from €10,900 (Nursery) to €14,900 (Grades 4–5). Notably, organic lunch is included. School transport (van service) is available but charged separately — which adds meaningful value compared to most international schools where these are paid separately. Application fee is €50, enrollment fee €1,500, annual re-enrollment €850.
What ages does Hypha cater for?
Hypha currently accepts children from ages 3 to 10 — Nursery through Grade 5. The school does not yet have a senior programme, so families should plan for a transition to another school for children aged 11+. For full-cycle options in the wider Lisbon area, see Oeiras International School or ISS Taguspark.
Where exactly is Hypha located?
The school is in Colares, in the municipality of Sintra — within the Sintra-Cascais Natural Park. It is approximately 40 minutes from central Lisbon by car. The school runs its own van transport service. The location is remote by design — the natural surroundings are integral to the school's philosophy.
